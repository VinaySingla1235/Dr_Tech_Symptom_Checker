Welcom to dr tech symptom detector. Here you can know the disease according to your symptoms it's an easy to use platform. In order to use it you first need to select your gender and age than you need to select the body part our human body map makes it easy to select body part we have seaparate maps for male & female, front & back. After clicking on any body part on map it will automatically select the specific body part and fetch symptoms according to it. After that you need to select your symptom(an symptom is the problem which you are facing in your body it's can be an indication of an disease). After selecting the symptom you need to click on next and it will fetch all the issues or diseases related to your symptom and after clicking on any disease you can get complete information about that disease which include descirption, medical condition, symptoms and treatement.
